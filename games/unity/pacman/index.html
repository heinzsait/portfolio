Author : Heinz Sait


Objective:

  • The objective is to eat all the dots in each maze and avoid the Ghost Gang or other Ghosts.


Logic Design and Implementation:

    • Use the arrow keys or swipe to move Pac-Man.
    • Red Ghost(Blinky), considered the de facto leader. He sticks close on Pac-Man's tail and always takes the fastest route to him, using A* path finding algorithm.
