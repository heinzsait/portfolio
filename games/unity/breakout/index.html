Author : Heinz Sait


Objective:

  • In this game, the player moves a paddle on the screen and bounces a ball. The objective is to destroy all the bricks in the top of the window.


Logic Design and Implementation:

    • Move the mouse horizontally to move the paddle left or right.
    • Hold down left mouse button to grab the ball, and press again to release.
    • Destroy all the bricks to win.
