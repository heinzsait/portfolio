Author : Heinz Sait


Objective:

  • Monster Ride is an endless runner, where the player runs as fast as he can and must collect mushrooms as he runs along the path avoiding obstacles.


Logic Design and Implementation:

    • Use left and right arrow keys to move left & right, up & down keys to jump & slide, and space to shoot.
    • The player must avoid hitting any obstacles, else he will take damage.
    • A boss is spawned after regular intervals, he will shoot you with a cannon and the player must evade his attacks and shoot him down.
