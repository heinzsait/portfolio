Author : Heinz Sait


Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to destroy as many as Enemy Ships.
  • The Enemy Ships and asteroids appear randomly from all directions.
  • The player gets 5 lives to score the highest.


Logic Design and Implementation:

    • Press the left mouse button to shoot & right mouse button to accelerate the ship.
    • There are 10 position from where randomly a position is selected.
    • From this position after certain period of time randomly a Enemy Ship or a asteroid is generated.
    • The Enemy Ships will chase and fire towards the player.
    • If the player gets hit by a bullet or an Enemy Ship or an asteroid the player will die.
