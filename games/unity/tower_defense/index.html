Author : Heinz Sait


Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to fend off the invading aliens.


Logic Design and Implementation:

    • There are 3 types of attack units.
    • The Bee is long range, Blue goo is short range, Green slime is short range tank with high damage
    • You can drag and drop units onto any tiles other than the first four tiles where the aliens spawn.
    • If any alien manages to break your defense and reach the leftmost tile, you lose.
