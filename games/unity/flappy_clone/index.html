Author : Heinz Sait


Objective:

  • FlappyClone is a game in which the player taps the screen to move upwards. The player must navigate the bird through a series of openings between pipes. Colliding with or getting near a pipe results in a game over.


Logic Design and Implementation:

    • FlappyClone contains one level that goes on until the player loses and that can be replayed, one way to earn points, one kind of obstacle which varies in height randomly.
    • You can either tap or press space to move the bird upwards.
