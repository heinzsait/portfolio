Author : Heinz Sait


Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to make a first person shooter.
  • There are 3 enemies and killing them is you mission.


Logic Design and Implementation:

    • If the player comes in line of sight of any enemy, it will shoot and follow you .
    • Once the player gets detected the enemy will follow you using waypoints.
    • The enemy uses Dijkstra's shortest path algorithm to find the optimal path to the player (No NavMesh used).
